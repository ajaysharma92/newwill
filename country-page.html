Canada Immigration

Express Entry was introduced by the Immigration Refugees Citizenship and Canada & CIC in 2015 for eradicating the flaws faced with the previous Immigration system.

Express Entry is the immigration system followed by the Canadian Government. Express Entry is an electronic following a point based system for evaluating a candidate for a Canadian immigration. The points are based on core human factors and a minimum 67 points out of 100 have to be secured to gain the eligibility.
