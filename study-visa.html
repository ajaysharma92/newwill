Canada Study Visa

Canada: The Education hub of the world:

In order for a foreign national to study in Canada, they require certain authorization. Canada encourages International students to come and study in Canada. There are several programs that have been introduced by the government to allow International Students to work while studying, work after they have completed their studies and to apply for permanent residence. Under the current immigration regime, a Canadian study permit is the surest possible way for foreign nationals to settle in Canada.

Lower Cost:

Choosing to student in Canada would save International Students a considerable amount of money. For instance, in a 2003 survey compiled by the Association of Commonwealth Universities, Canada offered the lowest tuition rates for foreign students compared to the U.K., Australia and New Zealand. Factor in that Canada has a lower cost of living making the financial advantages even greater. This is a great opportunity to make Canada their home by obtaining a local education. The Canadian Immigration program is very helpful to International students. Study Permit Holders may gain additional points when they apply for Permanent Residence. These Applicants are expected to be able to demonstrate sufficient financial capabilities to fund their studies and themselves while they are in Canada. Thus this is seen as quite an expensive option.

Eligibility Criteria to study in Canada:
  • Acceptance by a designated learning institution in Canada.
  • In order to prove fluency in English language, it is imperative to give exams for any internationally recognized English proficiency tests – TOEFL or IELTS. The minimum scores vary from university to university, and hence students may check the minimum requirement at the university of their choice.
  • Evidence of sufficient funds to pay:
    • Tuition Fees
    • Living expenses for yourself and any family members who accompany you to Canada and
    • Return transportation for yourself and any family members who comes with you to Canada
  • You must be a law-abiding citizen with no criminal record and not be a risk to the security of Canada. Present a police certificate to the visa issuing authority.
  • You must be in good health and willing to complete a medical examination, if needed.
  • You must satisfy an immigration officer that you will leave Canada at the end of your authorized stay.
